## Research

### Low-Cost, High performance Actuator Design: 3D Printed Water-Cooled QDD Actuator

I designed, manufactured, and tested a 3D printed **Quasi-Direct Drive (QDD)** actuator that achieves near-metal torque (peak and continuous) levels using a custom water-cooling loop. The project is submitted for Terra STEM Fair 2026.

* **Continuous Torque:** Optimized thermal management to double the continuous torque limit compared to previous 3D-printed actuators.
* **Design:** Custom cycloidal planetary gearbox (7.2:1) with a custom outrunner BLDC motor. Calculated bearing loads and optimized core actuator components.
